About Me

Hi. My name is Max Diaz. I am returning to school to obtain my B.S in computer science. I have an interest in both frontend and backend development. While initially began school pursuing a Civil/Mechanical Engineering, I discovered my deep fascination for computer science. Years of experience as a lead technician in production engineering have equipped me with invaluable skills, such as problem-solving, attention to detail, and effective teamwork. I am excited to leverage this experience as I dig further into the field of computer science.

Currently, I am working on projects that allow me to apply my skills in both frontend and backend development. This hands-on experience has been instrumental in expanding my knowledge of programming languages and cutting-edge technologies. I relish the opportunity to tackle challenges and explore innovative solutions within this dynamic field.

The future in the computer science field shows tremendous opportunities, and I eagerly anticipate the endless possibilities it offers. I am excited to embark on this journey and contribute my skills and enthusiasm to making a meaningful impact in the field.
